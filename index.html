Why Ride Share Eth

Built for the way Addis moves

Anbessa and Sheger buses fill up early, blue minibuses cost more, and private cabs stretch budgets. Ride Share Eth matches drivers already on the road with riders nearby so trips stay affordable.

  • Post your route 20–30 minutes before departure.
  • Riders bid for seats along the way—Bole Bulbula to Megenagna and beyond.
  • Transparent feedback: drivers and passengers see each other's ratings.
  • Pricing that makes sense: birr-per-kilometer, usually 60–70 birr for popular corridors.
